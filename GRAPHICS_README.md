# Graphics & Image Guidelines

To ensure your store looks perfect, please use the following dimensions for images in the custom sections.

## Feature Scroll Section

This section has a full-width background and 4 feature cards.

### **Background Image**
*   **Dimensions**: `1920px (width) x 800px (height)`
*   **Format**: JPG or WEBP (optimized)
*   **Notes**: The center of the image is the safe zone. Sides might be cropped on smaller screens.

### **Feature Cards**
*   **Dimensions**: `300px (width) x 400px (height)`
*   **Aspect Ratio**: 3:4 (Vertical Portrait)
*   **Format**: JPG or PNG
*   **Notes**: These images fill the card completely. Avoid placing important text too close to the edges.

---

## Category Section

This section displays circular category icons.

### **Category Icons**
*   **Dimensions**: `96px x 96px` (or larger square like `200px x 200px` for retina displays)
*   **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (Square)
*   **Format**: PNG (transparent background recommended) or JPG
*   **Notes**: These are cropped into circles automatically. Ensure the subject is centered.

### **Section Background (Optional)**
*   **Dimensions**: `1920px (width) x 600px (height)`
*   **Format**: JPG
